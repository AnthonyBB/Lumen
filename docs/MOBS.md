# Lumen Bestiary

47 mob archetypes across the 8 biomes. Each biome location spawns mobs of a
single difficulty tier, and each tier maps to a level band:

| Tier | Locations | Level band |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Easy | first location of a biome | 1–20 |
| Medium | second location | 21–55 |
| Hard | third location | 56–100 |

Within a location, later encounters on the path spawn from higher slices of the
band, so the last fight is always the toughest. The sample HP / Attack / Speed
columns below are computed at each tier's midpoint level (11 / 38 / 78).
✨ = magical attacker (attack scales with Intelligence instead of Strength).

## How stats scale

Every archetype has six **stat weights** (1–10) that describe its identity:
Strength, Constitution, Dexterity, Intelligence, Spirit, and Speed. Trolls have
high Strength/Constitution but low Speed; wolves are the opposite. When a mob
spawns at level **L** (1–100), its battle stats are derived from the weights:

```
maxHp   = round((20 + L × 6)   × Constitution / 5)
attack  = round((4  + L × 1.2) × Strength / 5)        ← Intelligence for casters (✨)
defense = round((2  + L × 0.8) × (Constitution + Strength) / 10)
speed   = round((10 + L × 0.5) × Speed / 5)
```

All stats rise monotonically with level; the weights set the slope, so a
high-CON slime out-tanks a low-CON wolf at every level.

### Speed and initiative

At the start of a battle, the player's speed is compared with the **fastest**
mob in the encounter. The faster side takes the first turn ("⚡ You strike
first!" / "⚠️ The enemy is faster!"). During the enemy turn, mobs act in
descending speed order. The default player speed is 25, which outruns most
easy-tier mobs but loses the opening move to swift high-level hunters like the
Shadow Panther or Deep Shark.

### XP rewards

Defeating a mob grants `10 + level × 2` XP, so hard-tier biomes pay far more
than starter zones.
